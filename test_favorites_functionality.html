Open the chat application at http://localhost:5173

Test steps:

  1. Navigate to the chat section

  3. Click "Add to Favorites" or "Remove from Favorites"
  4. Verify the star icon appears/disappears next to the contact name
  5. Check that the contact appears in the "Favorites" tab
  6. In the Favorites tab, click the "Clear All" button
  7. Confirm the action when prompted
  8. Verify that all favorites are cleared and the list is empty
